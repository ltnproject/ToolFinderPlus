How it works

Three steps.
Zero friction.

No installation. No account. Open the app, point at text, get results. That's the whole flow.

01
📷
Point & Scan

Aim your camera at any text — a tutorial, a task description, printed notes. Hit the scan button or press Space.

02
🔍
OCR Extraction

Tesseract.js reads the text locally in your browser. No image ever leaves your device. Supports 13 languages.

03
🔧
Tool Matching

Keywords are scored against a curated tool database. Top matches are ranked and linked instantly. Tap to open.
